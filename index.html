Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. The classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. Add water or milk for fluffiness.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the edges set but the center is slightly runny, add fillings on one half.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette over and cook for another minute before serving.

Nutrition

Values per serving without additional fillings:

Calories 277 kcal
Carbs 0 g
Protein 20 g
Fat 22 g
